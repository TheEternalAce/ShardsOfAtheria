Shards of Atheria is an ambitious project where I aim to bring a cohesive story into Terraria, while also adding fun and interesting content. Currently it only adds content and there isn't much lore.

Join the Discord/Guilded server to report bugs, see spoilers and other things. Invite links in the Forum thread.
Visit the [url=https://forums.terraria.org/index.php?threads/shards-of-atheria-mod.112953/] Forum [/url] thread for more info.
